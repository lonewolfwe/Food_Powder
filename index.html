Government Support for Your Venture

PMFME Scheme

The Pradhan Mantri Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme provides financial, technical, and business support. Key feature includes a credit-linked capital subsidy of 35% of the eligible project cost, with a maximum ceiling of ‚Çπ10 lakh per unit.

PMKSY (CEFPPC)

The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) includes the 'Creation/Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities' (CEFPPC) sub-scheme. It offers grants-in-aid for setting up new units, providing 35% of the project cost for general areas and 50% for difficult areas/aspirational districts.
